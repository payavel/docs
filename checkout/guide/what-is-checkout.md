# What is Checkout?

Payavel Checkout is the overarching package for handling **Revenue Capture** in your Laravel application.

It securely processes and orchestrates one-time payments and authorizations across multiple gateways (like Stripe, Adyen, and Braintree) using a single, unified API. Built on top of Payavel's core orchestration layer, it entirely abstracts away the specific implementations of your underlying payment providers.
