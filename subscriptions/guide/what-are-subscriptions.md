# What are Subscriptions?

Payavel Subscriptions is an upcoming package in our end-to-end commerce suite, focused on **Revenue Retention**.

This package will empower you to effortlessly handle recurring billing, lifecycle renewals, dunning, and prorations. It provides a unified orchestration layer over various providers, abstracting away the complexities of subscription management.

*This section of the documentation will be updated once the Subscriptions package is fully released.*
