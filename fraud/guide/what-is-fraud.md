# What is Fraud?

Payavel Fraud is an upcoming package in our end-to-end commerce suite, focused on **Revenue Assurance**.

This package will protect your business with built-in fraud prevention integrations that shield your revenue stream. It abstracts the integration with various risk evaluation APIs, allowing you to establish a robust and provider-agnostic defense against chargebacks and fraudulent behavior.

*This section of the documentation will be updated once the Fraud package is fully released.*
